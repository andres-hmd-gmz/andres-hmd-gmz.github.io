About me

I am assistant professor in the Department of Mathematics at CUNEF Universidad in Madrid, Spain.

I am mathematician from Facultad de Ciencias of the Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México. I wrote my PhD Thesis "Actions on Spaces with Bounded Curvature" with supervision of Dr. Fernando Galaz García.

In geometry I have found a way of thinking and what interests me most is seeing how we can obtain topological information from Riemannian geometry (connection, geodesics or curvature) or metric geometry (angles, distances or comparison).

By the way, I've always had a special fondness for spheres.
